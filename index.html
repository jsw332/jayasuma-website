Personal Info

Dr. G Jaya Suma is an accomplished academician with a B. Tech, M. Tech, and Ph.D. in Computer Science and Data Mining. With 21 years of experience, she has held teaching and administrative roles, including being a Professor and HOD of IT at JNTUK. Currently a Professor in IT, she also directs Faculty Development and Industry Institute Interaction Placements & Training at JNTUGV, Vizianagaram. Her career reflects dedication to education, research, and industry collaboration.
